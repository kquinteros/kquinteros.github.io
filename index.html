Welcome Kevin Quinteros

Welcome to my website. My name is Kevin Quinteros. I am a postdoctoral researcher who is passionate about studying plant-insect interactions. Using a combination of field research and genomic techniques, I investigate the important processes that produce and maintain coevolutionary interactions between plants and insects. My research seeks to provide a deeper understanding of the ecological and evolutionary dynamics that drive these interactions.
